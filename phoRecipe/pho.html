Pho Recipe

Pho Ingredients

  • 5 pounds beef bones
  • 2 medium onions, quartered
  • 2 heads of garlic, halved crosswise
  • 2 (2-in) pieces of ginger, sliced lengthwise
  • 6 whole star anise
  • 12 whole cloves
  • 2 whole cinnamon sticks
  • 2 black cardamom pods
  • 2 tablespoons fennel seeds
  • 2 tablespoons coriander seeds
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2/3 cup fish sauce
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 pound dried pho noodles
  • 1/3 pound beef sirloin, thinly cut
  • garnishes: chooped cilantro, sriracha, basil, beansprouts

Instructions

  1. preheat the oven to 450F
  2. place the beef bones, onions, garlic and ginger on a pan and roast for 30 mins
  3. transfer the roasted bones and vegetables to a large pot. Add the star anise, cloves, cinnamon sticks, cardamom pods (if using), fennel seeds, coriander seeds, salt, fish sauce, and sugar. Cover with water and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 6-8 hours, or until the broth is rich and flavorful.
  5. Strain the broth through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean pot. Discard the bones and spices.
  6. cook the noodles according to package directions
  7. To assemble the pho, place a handful of noodles in each bowl. Top with the thinly sliced beef. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and beef.
  8. Garnish with cilantro, basil, bean sprouts, lime wedges, chili slices, hoisin sauce, and sriracha to taste.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
